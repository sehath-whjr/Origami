Interesting Facts About Camels

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication amongst chameleons.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting Facts About Camels.

  • Camels' ears are furry.
  • Camels can move easily across sand because of its specially designed feet.
  • When they find water, they drink as much as possible.
